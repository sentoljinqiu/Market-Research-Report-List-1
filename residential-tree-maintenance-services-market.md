<p><ul><li>Corrective Maintenance</li><li>Preventive Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential tree maintenance services encompass two primary market types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing immediate issues such as disease, damage, or safety hazards by pruning, removing dead branches, or treating infected trees. In contrast, preventive maintenance focuses on proactive care, including routine inspections, fertilization, and shaping, to maintain tree health and mitigate future problems. Both approaches are essential for ensuring the longevity and aesthetic appeal of residential landscapes, promoting safety and enhancing property value.</p></p>
