<p><strong>The Metal Bellows Expansion Joints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal bellows expansion joints are categorized into two primary types: Type I and Type II. </p><p>Type I expansion joints are designed for axial movements, accommodating thermal expansions and contractions in piping systems. They typically consist of a single bellows element. </p><p>Type II expansion joints, on the other hand, are engineered to handle both axial and lateral movements, featuring multiple bellows or additional components for enhanced flexibility. This allows them to address more complex alignment and thermal stress issues in various industrial applications.</p></p>
