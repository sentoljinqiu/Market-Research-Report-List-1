<p><strong>The 1,4 Bitanediol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reppe process</li><li>Davy process</li><li>Butadiene process</li><li>Propylene oxide process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>1,4-Butanediol (BDO) is produced through various processes, each impacting the market differently. The Reppe process involves the reaction of acetylene with formaldehyde and hydrogen, resulting in BDO with high purity. The Davy process uses a catalytic method to produce BDO from maleic anhydride. The Butadiene process derives BDO from butadiene through a series of reactions, while the Propylene oxide process utilizes propylene oxide as a precursor. Each method influences production costs, supply chains, and market dynamics based on efficiency and raw material availability.</p></p>
