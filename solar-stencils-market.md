<p><strong>The Solar Stencils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Electrode Screen</li><li>Back Electrode Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar stencils market is primarily divided into two categories: positive electrode screens and back electrode screens. Positive electrode screens are used to create conductive patterns on the front side of solar cells, enhancing efficiency by facilitating light absorption. Back electrode screens, on the other hand, are applied to the rear side, helping to minimize resistance and optimize energy output. Both types are essential in the production of high-performance solar panels, contributing to advancements in photovoltaic technology.</p></p>
