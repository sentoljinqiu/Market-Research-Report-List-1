<p><strong>The Vacuum Deposition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Evaporation"</li><li>"Sputtering"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum deposition market comprises two primary types: evaporation and sputtering. Evaporation involves heating materials in a vacuum to produce vapor, which condenses on a substrate, forming thin films. It's commonly used for metals and some organic compounds. Sputtering, on the other hand, utilizes plasma to eject particles from a target material onto the substrate, enabling precise layering and control over film properties. Both methods serve various industries, including electronics, optics, and coatings, facilitating advanced material applications.</p></p>
