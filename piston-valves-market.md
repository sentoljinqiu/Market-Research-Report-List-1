<p><p>The piston valves market is segmented into four main types: E-Type, S-Type, F-Type, and LH-Type. F-Type valves are designed for fast actuation and precise throttling, enhancing operational efficiency. LH-Type valves are known for their low hydraulic resistance, making them suitable for high-flow conditions. Each type caters to specific industry needs and performance criteria.</p></p>
