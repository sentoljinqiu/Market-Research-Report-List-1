<p><ul><li>Points-based Loyalty Programs</li><li>Tier-based Loyalty Programs</li><li>Subscription-based Loyalty Programs</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retail loyalty programs can be categorized into several market types. Points-based programs reward customers with points for purchases, which can be redeemed for discounts or rewards. Tier-based programs create levels of membership based on customer spending, offering increasing benefits as customers ascend tiers. Subscription-based programs charge a fee for exclusive perks and rewards, enhancing loyalty. Other types may include cashback programs, personalized offers, or coalition loyalty programs that combine benefits from multiple retailers, appealing to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
