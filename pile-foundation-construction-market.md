<p><ul><li>Hammering</li><li>Vibration Method</li><li>Press Method</li><li>Water Jet</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pile foundation construction market consists of various methods, each suited for different soil conditions and project requirements. The hammering method involves driving piles into the ground using a heavy weight, ensuring stability. The vibration method uses vibration to reduce soil resistance, facilitating easier pile placement. The press method involves hydraulically pushing piles into the ground, minimizing disturbance. Water jetting employs high-pressure water to liquefy soil, allowing for the installation of piles. Other methods may include augering and diaphragm wall techniques.</p></p>
