<p><p>The biodegradable recycled thermoplastics market encompasses various forms, including flakes, pellets, and granulates. Flakes are small, flat pieces that result from breaking down larger plastic materials, making them easy to transport and process. Pellets are uniform, rounded shapes that can be easily melted and molded for manufacturing applications. Granulates refer to larger, granular particles that serve as a versatile feedstock for producing new plastic products. Each type plays a crucial role in sustainable plastic production and waste management practices.</p></p>
