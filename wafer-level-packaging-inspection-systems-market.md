<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging (WLP) Inspection Systems encompass various technologies to ensure quality in semiconductor manufacturing. Optical-based systems utilize light to assess surface features and defects, providing high-resolution imaging and real-time data analysis. In contrast, infrared-type systems detect thermal properties and material defects by measuring infrared radiation, which is particularly effective for identifying issues in die attach and interconnects. Both types play essential roles in enhancing the reliability and performance of WLP, catering to the needs of evolving electronics applications.</p></p>
