<p><p>On-site milling encompasses various market types including linear, gantry, and orbital milling. Linear milling involves moving equipment in straight lines for precise machining of large components. Gantry milling utilizes a bridge-like structure, allowing for flexible movement and larger workpieces, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Orbital milling features rotational cutting, effectively managing complex geometries and surfaces. Each method is tailored for specific project requirements, emphasizing efficiency, accuracy, and adaptability in diverse industrial contexts.</p></p>
