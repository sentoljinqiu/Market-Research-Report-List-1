<p><p>The photoplethysmography (PPG) biosensors market includes various types of sensors used for monitoring cardiovascular health. ECG sensors measure the electrical activity of the heart, providing insights into heart rhythm and potential abnormalities. PPG sensors, on the other hand, use light absorption techniques to assess blood volume changes in microvascular tissues, enabling heart rate and blood oxygen level monitoring. Other types encompass additional biometric sensors like impedance sensors and temperature sensors, expanding the scope of health monitoring technologies.</p></p>
