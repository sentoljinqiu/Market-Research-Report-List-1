<p><strong>The EOOE Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EOOE (Electrical Over Optical Ethernet) converters market is segmented into two primary types: single mode and multimode. Single mode converters use a single fiber strand to transmit data over long distances, offering higher bandwidth and lower attenuation, making them ideal for extensive networks. In contrast, multimode converters employ multiple strands for shorter distances, supporting higher data rates over shorter runs, which makes them suitable for local area networks and data centers. Each type serves distinct networking needs and applications.</p></p>
