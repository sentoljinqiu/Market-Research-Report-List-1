<p><p>The orthodontic band market features three primary types: metal, silica gel, and ceramics. Metal bands, typically made of stainless steel, are strong and durable, widely used for traditional braces. Silica gel bands offer flexibility and comfort, making them popular for patients with sensitivity. Ceramic bands, designed for aesthetics, blend with the teeth, providing a discreet option for those seeking a more visually appealing treatment. Each type serves unique needs, catering to diverse patient preferences and treatment requirements in orthodontics.</p></p>
