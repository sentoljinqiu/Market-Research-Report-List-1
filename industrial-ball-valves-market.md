<p><p>The industrial ball valves market consists of various types, each designed for specific applications and installation methods. Flanged ball valves feature flanges for easy bolting to pipelines, ensuring robust connections. Threaded ball valves utilize male and female threads for straightforward attachment, making them suitable for smaller systems. Socket-weld ball valves are designed for welding into the piping system, providing strong, leak-proof joints ideal for high-pressure environments. Each type serves distinct operational needs within various industrial sectors.</p></p>
