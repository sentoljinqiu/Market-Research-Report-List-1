<p><p>The electrochemical generator market encompasses various fuel cell types, each with distinct characteristics. Alkaline fuel cells utilize alkaline electrolyte and are known for high efficiency. Phosphoric acid fuel cells operate at higher temperatures, making them suitable for stationary applications. Solid oxide fuel cells have a high efficiency and can use various fuels, while molten carbonate fuel cells operate at elevated temperatures and can process carbon dioxide. Proton exchange membrane fuel cells are lightweight and ideal for vehicles, emphasizing versatility across applications.</p></p>
