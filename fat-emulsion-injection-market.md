<p><p>The fat emulsion injection market is categorized into single-chamber and multi-chamber types. Single-chamber emulsions contain all ingredients in one chamber, simplifying preparation and administration, making them suitable for immediate use. In contrast, multi-chamber emulsions separate components, allowing for prolonged stability and tailored nutrient delivery. This type requires mixing before administration, which enhances flexibility in formulations. Both types cater to different clinical needs, offering options based on stability, convenience, and specific nutritional requirements for patients requiring parenteral nutrition.</p></p>
