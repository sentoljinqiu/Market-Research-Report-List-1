<p><ul><li>Two Level Parallel</li><li>Three Level Parallel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Onshore wind power converters are essential for transforming wind energy into usable electricity. The Two Level Parallel market involves converters that use a simplified architecture with two voltage levels for efficient power conversion, typically offering lower costs and reduced complexity. In contrast, the Three Level Parallel market employs a more advanced design with three voltage levels, enhancing power quality and reducing harmonic distortion. Both types cater to the specific needs of wind farms, balancing efficiency, cost, and performance in energy conversion.</p></p>
