<p><p>The fireproof glass door market encompasses three main types: Fixed Type, Movable Type, and Automatic Inductive Type. Fixed Type doors are stationary installations designed to resist fire while providing visibility. Movable Type doors, such as sliding or folding models, offer flexible access and can be closed during a fire. Automatic Inductive Type doors utilize sensors for hands-free operation, enhancing convenience and safety while still maintaining fire-resistance features. Each type serves distinct functional and safety needs in various environments.</p></p>
