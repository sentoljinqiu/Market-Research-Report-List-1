<p><p>The Polyethylene Pipe Coating market is segmented into internal and external coatings, each serving distinct purposes. Internal coatings enhance the flow characteristics and reduce friction within the pipe, protecting against corrosion and scaling. External coatings provide a protective layer against environmental factors, mechanical damage, and corrosion, extending the pipe's lifespan. Both types are crucial for maintaining the integrity and efficiency of pipelines in various industries, including water distribution, oil and gas, and construction.</p></p>
