<p><strong>The Acute Kidney Injury Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prerenal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Postrenal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Intrinsic Renal Acute Kidney Injury</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) treatment market is categorized into three main types: prerenal, postrenal, and intrinsic renal acute kidney injuries. Prerenal AKI is often due to reduced blood flow to the kidneys, and treatment focuses on restoring adequate circulation. Postrenal AKI results from obstruction in the urinary tract, requiring interventions to relieve blockages. Intrinsic renal AKI involves direct damage to kidney tissues, necessitating approaches to promote healing and manage underlying conditions. Each type demands tailored therapeutic strategies for effective management.</p></p>
