<p><ul><li>Absorbable Type</li><li>Non-Absorbable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymer ligation clips market is categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable. Absorbable polymer ligation clips are designed to dissolve over time, reducing the need for future surgical removals and minimizing long-term foreign body presence. In contrast, non-absorbable polymer clips remain in the body indefinitely, providing permanent ligation solutions for various surgical applications. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on surgical goals, patient needs, and the specific medical procedures being performed.</p></p>
