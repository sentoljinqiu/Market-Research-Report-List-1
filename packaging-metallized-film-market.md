<p><p>The packaging metallized film market features several key types, including PET, CPP, and BOPP films. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) films offer high tensile strength and excellent barrier properties, making them ideal for food and electronic packaging. CPP (cast polypropylene) films provide good moisture resistance and sealability, suitable for flexible packaging applications. BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) films are known for their clarity and strength, often used in labels and snack packaging. Each type serves specific needs in the packaging industry.</p></p>
