<p><strong>The Cell Viability Dye Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DNA Binding Dye</li><li>Amine Reactive Dye</li><li>Enzyme Activated Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell viability dye market comprises three main types: DNA binding dyes, amine reactive dyes, and enzyme activated dyes. DNA binding dyes intercalate with cellular DNA, allowing for the assessment of cell membrane integrity and viability. Amine reactive dyes target specific amine groups on proteins, providing insights into viable cell populations. Enzyme activated dyes release fluorescent signals upon enzymatic action, often used for live/dead discrimination. Together, these dyes facilitate accurate analysis in various biological and clinical applications.</p></p>
