<p><ul><li>Ball Screw Driven Electric Cylinders</li><li>Acme Screw Driven Electric Cylinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric cylinders are categorized into two main types: ball screw driven and acme screw driven. Ball screw driven electric cylinders utilize a ball screw mechanism for high efficiency and precision, providing smooth motion with minimal backlash. They are ideal for applications requiring fast and accurate positioning. In contrast, acme screw driven electric cylinders rely on an acme screw, offering a simpler design with lower cost and versatility but typically at the expense of speed and precision.</p></p>
