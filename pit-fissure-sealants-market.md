<p><p>Pit and fissure sealants are dental products used to prevent caries in children and adolescents. The market primarily comprises two types: resin-based sealants and glass ionomer sealants. Resin-based sealants are favored for their durability and aesthetic appeal, effectively sealing deep grooves in teeth. Glass ionomer sealants, on the other hand, release fluoride, providing additional protection against decay and are more suitable for patients with a high risk of caries. Each type addresses specific dental needs and preferences.</p></p>
