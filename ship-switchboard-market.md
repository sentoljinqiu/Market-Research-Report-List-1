<p><strong>The Ship Switchboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LV Ship Switchboard</li><li>MV & HV Ship Switchboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship switchboard market is categorized into three main types: LV (Low Voltage), MV (Medium Voltage), and HV (High Voltage) ship switchboards. LV ship switchboards manage electrical systems operating up to 1,000 volts, typically powering lighting and auxiliary systems. MV ship switchboards, functioning between 1,000 and 35,000 volts, are used for main propulsion and power distribution. HV ship switchboards, exceeding 35,000 volts, are crucial for large vessels and offshore platforms, handling high power demands efficiently and safely.</p></p>
