<p><p>The vacuum filtration equipment market includes three main types: mechanical, electronic, and gas-phase. Mechanical vacuum filtration relies on physical processes using pumps and filters to separate solids from liquids. Electronic systems incorporate automated controls for optimized filtration, enhancing efficiency and consistency. Gas-phase filtration focuses on removing contaminants from gases, often employed in air purification and industrial applications. Each type serves specific industries and applications, offering tailored solutions to meet varying filtration needs in laboratories, manufacturing, and environmental management.</p></p>
