<p><p>The train door system market can be categorized into automatic and semi-automatic systems. Semi-automatic systems require some manual operation from train staff, providing flexibility in certain situations while still offering automated features. Both types aim to improve passenger flow and safety, with automatic systems being prevalent in modern trains, while semi-automatic systems are often found in older models.</p></p>
