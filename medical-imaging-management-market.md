<p><strong>The Medical Imaging Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vendor Neutral Archive System(VNA)</li><li>Picture Archiving and Communications System (PACS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Imaging Management market encompasses systems that facilitate the storage and management of medical imaging data. Vendor Neutral Archive Systems (VNA) provide a centralized solution for storing images from various imaging devices, ensuring interoperability across different platforms. In contrast, Picture Archiving and Communications Systems (PACS) focus on the acquisition, storage, and distribution of medical images within healthcare facilities. While PACS is device-specific, VNA promotes data accessibility across multiple vendors, enhancing collaboration and improving patient care.</p></p>
