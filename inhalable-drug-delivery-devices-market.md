<p><p>The inhalable drug delivery devices market comprises several types, including Pressurized Metered Dose Inhalers (PMDIs), which deliver a specific amount of medication with each puff, offering portability and ease of use. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into aerosol for inhalation, often used for severe respiratory conditions. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) deliver medication in a powdered form, requiring patient inhalation to activate the dose. Other devices may include soft mist inhalers and breath-activated devices, enhancing treatment options for respiratory diseases.</p></p>
