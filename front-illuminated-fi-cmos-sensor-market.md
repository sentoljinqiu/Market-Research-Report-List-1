<p><strong>The Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS</li><li>Active Pixel Sensor CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Sensor (APS). PPS utilizes simple pixel structures, primarily focusing on low-cost applications with minimal features, which often results in lower image quality. In contrast, APS incorporates amplifying transistors within each pixel, enabling higher image quality, better sensitivity, and enhanced performance in various lighting conditions. This makes APS sensors more suitable for advanced imaging applications, including smartphones and digital cameras.</p></p>
