<p><p>The prosthetic liners market comprises three main types: silicone liners, polyurethane (PUR) liners, and thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) liners. Silicone liners offer excellent cushioning and durability, providing enhanced comfort and skin protection. PUR liners are known for their resilience and flexibility, making them suitable for active users. TPE liners combine the properties of rubber and plastic, offering a lightweight and adaptable solution. Each type serves distinct user needs, focusing on comfort, durability, and ease of maintenance in prosthetic applications.</p></p>
