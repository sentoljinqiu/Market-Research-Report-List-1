<p><p>The anemia therapeutics market encompasses several types, each addressing specific anemia conditions. Sickle cell anemia treatments aim to manage pain and prevent complications, often using hydroxyurea and blood transfusions. Pernicious anemia therapies involve vitamin B12 replacement to counter deficiencies. Hemolytic anemia treatments target the underlying causes, including corticosteroids and immunosuppressants, to manage the destruction of red blood cells. Overall, the market is expanding with ongoing research and novel therapies.</p></p>
