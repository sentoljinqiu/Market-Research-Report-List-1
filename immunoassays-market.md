<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Immunoassays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enzyme Immunoassay</li><li>Fluorescence Immunoassay</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Nephelometric Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunoassays market comprises various types, each utilizing different detection methods. Enzyme immunoassays leverage enzyme-substrate reactions for signal amplification. Fluorescence immunoassays use fluorescent labels for sensitive detection. Chemiluminescence immunoassays generate light through chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity. Radioimmunoassays utilize radioactive isotopes to quantify substances, though their use has declined due to safety concerns. Lastly, nephelometric immunoassays measure scattered light to determine particle concentration, commonly applied in clinical diagnostics and research settings. Each type serves unique applications in healthcare and laboratory environments.</p></p>
