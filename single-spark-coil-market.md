<p><ul><li>Soft Iron Core</li><li>Hard Iron Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Spark Coil market is primarily segmented into two types based on core material: Soft Iron Core and Hard Iron Core. Soft Iron Core coils are characterized by their high magnetic permeability, providing efficient energy transfer and quick response times, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid ignition. In contrast, Hard Iron Core coils feature enhanced durability and resistance to demagnetization, which makes them ideal for high-performance environments. Each type caters to specific applications in automotive and industrial sectors, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
