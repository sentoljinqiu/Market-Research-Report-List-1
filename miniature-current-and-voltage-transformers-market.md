Conversely, MVTs provide voltage measurement and monitoring with minimal footprint, ideal for compact installations. Both types are crucial in sectors like renewable energy, automation, and smart grids, facilitating precise monitoring and control while ensuring equipment safety and operational reliability.</p></p>
