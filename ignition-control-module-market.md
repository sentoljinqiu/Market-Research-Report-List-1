<p><strong>The Ignition Control Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Sensor</li><li>Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ignition Control Module (ICM) market can be categorized into two main types based on sensor technology: inductive sensors and Hall sensors. Inductive sensors operate by detecting changes in magnetic fields, providing reliable ignition timing through electromagnetic induction, commonly used in traditional ignition systems. Hall sensors, on the other hand, leverage Hall effect principles to accurately sense the position of the rotor, ensuring precise timing for modern ignition systems. Both types cater to diverse automotive applications, enhancing engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
