The U-shape parabolic trough design features two symmetric parabolic mirrors that effectively concentrate sunlight, optimizing land use and energy capture. In contrast, the V-shape parabolic trough configuration employs angled mirrors to direct sunlight onto a central receiver, enhancing efficiency in varied terrains. Both designs cater to different market needs, balancing efficiency and cost-effectiveness in solar energy production.</p></p>
